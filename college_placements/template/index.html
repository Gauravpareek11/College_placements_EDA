College Placements

I have Performed Exploratory Data Analysis on College Placements Dataset and then created a Machie Learning Model to Predict whether a student will be placed or not.

Dataset Overview

A University Announced Its On-Campus Placement Records For The Engineering Course. The Data Is From The Years 2013 And 2014. The Following Is The College Placements Data Compiled Over 2 years.
The Dataset has 8 columns:

  • Numeric Features: Age, Internships, CGPA, Hostel, HistoryOfBacklogs, PlacedOrNot.
  • Categorical Features: Gender, Stream

Exploratory Data Analysis

Density and histogram plot on Age and CGPA column

Count Plot

Bar plot on column with respect to Gender

Conclusions

  • Male candidates have more backlog than females
  • Females have more tendency of getting internships
  • Meadian age of candidates getting placed 21.0
  • Day Scholars slightly higher chance of getting placed
  • Students from EC branch have the max chances of getting placed
    • followed by IT by a narrow margin and then CS and then Electrical.
  • Females have more tendency to stay in hostel than males
  • Doing internships increases your chance of getting placed
  • Chances of getting placed for females and males are almost same
